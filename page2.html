Songs That Remind Me of
South Africa
by The Kiffness




The one think I miss the most about South Africa is the food. This song is the South African Anthem for the best foods there are to eat. Boereworse is like an Italian sausage but with different seasoning.
The tast is unlike anything I have had here in the US.
Chappies define my childhood, I have so many memories of going to the shop up the road by my school to get some with my pocket money.



One of the most vivid memories I have of living in South Africa is the sound of the Hadeda,
which is the "turkey"of South Africa.
They make the most obnoxious annoying sound ever.




Along with all of the amazing food and nature of the country, there are some downsides to living there. I remember many night with no power for "loadshedding" which is when the entire country has no electricity to save electricity. Things like think make be greatful to not live in a third world country anymore but in a weird way I miss it.


Although South African is a distant memory for me now, it still holds a very speascial place in my heart and I will forever be South African at heart.
